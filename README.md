# NextEra Energy (nextera-energy)

NextEra Energy, Inc. is a Fortune 500 American energy company and one of the largest electric utility holding companies in the United States. It is the world's largest generator of renewable energy from the wind and sun, and a world leader in battery storage. NextEra Energy operates principally through its two subsidiaries, Florida Power & Light (FPL) and NextEra Energy Resources.
